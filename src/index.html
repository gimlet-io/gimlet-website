Deploy in minutes

Preflight

Make sure your CI builds and uploads a Docker image to the registry

Your CI verifies your app and builds a Docker image, but does not deploy to Kubernetes anymore. Gimlet does. It picks up Github statuses and kicks in right after your CI is done.

Configure your app

Enable your repo and answer a few questions

Kubernetes manifests all look alike. Skip the boilerplate, set only what is unique to your app!

Gimlet has good defaults, so most likely you click enable or change only a few things. You don't like clicking? Set these values in code, in the .gimlet.yml file.

Trigger a deployment

Push a commit

or promote a commit to an environment.

Gimlet brings non trivial benefits

Rollbacks and disaster recovery

Day 2 considerations like rollbacks, backups and disaster recovery are available in Gimlet. Thanks to the GitOps approach.

Preview environments

Gimlet comes with per branch preview environments, with customizable branch patterns and cleanup strategy.

Uniform deployments

Gimlet privdes a master template, so your apps are deployed identically. Making SRE life easier.

Best in class app dashboard for Kubernetes

Gimlet brings app specific dashboards that work on mobile. No more inventory view. See only what you care about.
